TRUNK OR TREAT

10-29-24

Join Marlboro High School's Trunk or Treat on October 29th (rain date: October 30th) from 4:00 PM to 5:30 PM at the school parking lot!

Free for ages 12 and under, with donations collected for the American Cancer Society. Enjoy music, games, face painting, and vendors like Feds on the Go and Goldy's Ice Cream.

Nut-free event, please ensure no nut-containing foods. Setup for participants at 3:00 PM, cleanup at 5:30 PM.
